# Version 34 Revision 12

Revision 12 builds directly on Revision 11 and preserves the sticky app dock, integrated pitcher selector, current batting-team logic, On Deck / In the Hole panels, scoring behavior, autosave, exports, and duplex PDF output.

## Change completed

- Simplified the live-field display on **iPhone and Android phones only**.
- Phone displays now hide all defensive-player position markers and names from the field picture.
- Each occupied base now shows only:
  - the base label (**1B**, **2B**, or **3B**)
  - the offensive runner’s uniform number
- Runner numbers continue to come from the team currently batting.
- The full defensive alignment and named runner display remain unchanged on **iPad and desktop**.
- Added a phone-landscape rule so the simplified field also applies when an iPhone or Android phone is rotated.
