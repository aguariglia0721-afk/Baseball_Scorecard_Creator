# Version 34 Revision 2 — Unified Notes Section

Version 34 remains the current build. This revision combines the two separate page-two notes areas into one larger **Game Notes & Events** section.

- Manual Game Notes and automatically recorded game-event notes now appear in the same section.
- The separate **Game Event Notes** box has been removed.
- The **Review Decision Log** remains separate for Replay and ABS rulings.
- Blank duplex PDFs now provide continuous writing lines in the unified notes area and the review log.
- Page one and all Version 33/34 scoring behavior are unchanged.
